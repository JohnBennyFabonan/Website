ABOUT

This digital tools website for Rosario was specifically designed for the Risen Christ Redeemer. Its goal is to improve digital marketing, attract more tourists, and provide reliable information on topics like accessibility, emergency numbers, other social media platforms, and activities. The website serves a dual purpose. It helps attract a broader range of tourists to the pilgrimage destination while also providing job opportunities for the local community of Rosario through increased tourism.

HISTORY

The Creation of the Risen Christ Redeemer:
The Risen Christ Redeemer and its grotto are a testament to faith and partnership in Rosario, Batangas. Between 1989 and 1999, a local businessman named Antonio Z. Luna started a special project to create a shrine. The municipal government of Rosario provided him with a 2,300-square-meter area at the base of Tombol Hill to make his vision a reality.

On this land, Mr. Luna built a grotto featuring the fourteen Stations of the Cross. These life-sized figures, crafted from cement, depict the solemn moments of the Holy Passion. Today, the grotto is a key religious attraction, drawing many visitors, particularly during the Lenten season.

A large statue of the Risen Christ stands above the grotto, at the very top of Tombol Hill, with its arms open in a welcoming gesture. This statue has since become a famous landmark of Rosario. Initially, the path to the top was a steep and challenging climb, with visitors having to use ropes tied to trees. To improve access for all ages, a staircase was built. This has made the spiritual journey to the top safe and easy for everyone.
